99% of our daily tasks can be automated
Did you know that the vast majority of tasks we do every day on our computers or smartphones can be automated? This is now possible thanks to artificial intelligence and automation. For example, a mailbot can read and respond to our emails. We give it our instructions and it can then make appointments or send offers for us.
